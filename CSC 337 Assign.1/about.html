Origins

Our store is a line of clothing aimed at Gen-Z with graphic tees, bags, & platforms for customers to fill their closets with Y2K inspired items.

Our clothing speaks to all boys, girls, and non-binary individuals. Everyone can find a piece of clothing that expresses their individuality.
We promote sustainability but in a subtle and creative way. We work with small and independent business to produce our clothing.
We think it is important to support our community and be aware of the ethical values of our partners.
